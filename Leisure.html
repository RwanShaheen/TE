  • Dahab

Red Sea Paradise

Description

Dahab is an Egyptian tourist city belonging to the South Sinai Governorate and is located on the Gulf of Aqaba, about 100 km away from the city of Sharm el-Sheikh and 87 km from the city of Nuweiba, and it was named after the color of its golden sand. The city is divided into two parts, the first is located in the south and is called the village of Al-Asla and is famous for the simple Bedouin life. The second section is located in the north and is considered the soul and pulse of the city because it includes commercial markets and places.

The most important tourist places in Dahab

The city is famous for its pristine beaches and natural diving sites rich in coral reefs.

Nabq Reserve

A tourist trip in Dahab is not completed without visiting the most important natural attractions in Dahab, due to its wild wealth. And a sea like no other in the reserves of the world. The reserve includes rare and endangered plants, birds and wild animals.

With extinction, their number reaches 134 species, of which 86 are extinct and unique species of fish and colourful coral reefs.

Bluehole area

Blue Hole is a very deep well inside the Red Sea. It is one of the city's tourist destinations, which is visited by adventure enthusiasts and diving professionals, who practice their hobby by penetrating a small arc at a depth of 100 meters, trying to breaking diving records.

Water sports in Dahab:

With all this number of coral reefs, rare sea creatures and the charm of its beaches, it is inconceivable that the Dahab region in Egypt is one of the most important areas for practicing water sports such as diving, windsurfing and kayaking, and among the most important diving sites on Dahab Island: Blue Hole Center, El Gouna Island, Isle Garden North Dahab, Al Waha. In addition to 30 internationally accredited centers For diving training, be sure not to miss an adventure like that during tourism in Dahab . .

Safari and camping in Dahab

In Egypt, the deserts and Sinai mountains in the tourist city of Dahab are an ideal and distinctive destination for adventurers who enjoy safari trips, camping and mountain climbing, where you can roam through them through cars and four-wheel-drive bikes with the experience of the Bedouin lifestyle and barbecue .

Tourism activities

The city is also distinguished by its distinguished tourism activities, as it is frequented by windsurfing and sailboat enthusiasts because of the wind speed, because it is surrounded by mountains on several sides, which causes the emergence of low pressure in places and high pressure in others. Skydiving and mountaineering enthusiasts, who want to enjoy the mountainous and desert nature and the mild climate for the city.

Dahab Seven Waves Festival

The Seven Waves of Dahab Festival was launched from 25 January 2015 until 31 January 2015 with the aim of stimulating tourism in the city with international and Arab partnerships. During which the Bedouins of the city presented exhibitions of Bedouin heritage, camel racing, and safari trips deep into the mountains. Diving and underwater search competitions were also organized to obtain a prize presented by the festival in the depths of water and local food.

Description

Snow City is one of the largest snow cities in Egypt, spread widely over an area of 6000 square meters with a temperature of less than -5 degrees Celsius. The entire center covers about 750 tons of fresh ice made from drinking water. It is environmentally friendly and edible as well. Snow City contains a group of games in the snowy nature that are impressive and fun.

As for the second floor, it contains corridors, halls, and internal rooms. The third floor includes a large room (the seat of Sultan Qaytbay) in which he sits to see the ships throughout the day from Alexandria, covered by a cross vault. There is also an oven on this floor for preparing wheat bread, as well as a mill for grinding grain for the soldiers residing in the castle. Sultan Qansuh al-Ghuri renewed the castle and increased its garrison. This castle was neglected during the Ottoman Caliphate period of Egypt.

  • Museum of Illusions

Fantasy City

Description

Have you ever thought about walking inside a swimming pool without getting wet, or walking through a vibrating tunnel, or standing on your head in a room? The answer lies in the Egyptian Museum of Illusions.

The Egyptian Museum of Illusions offers a unique entertainment experience with illusion games that test visitors' senses and minds. It is the first of its kind in Egypt and aims to simplify complex concepts through creative and entertaining educational facts. The museum features around 70 games based on visual, sensory, and mental illusions, as well as maze-like rooms and a gift shop with brain-teaser games and unique decorations.

  1. AMES Room

    2. The Ames Room appears to be a regular room from the outside, but in reality, it is creatively distorted. It deceives the senses and makes you appear gigantic at the front of the room and dwarfed at the back, giving the illusion that a person can grow and shrink just by changing their position. This demonstrates the ingenuity of the design. It is especially amusing to have designated spots for photographers and visitors, resulting in wonderfully humorous photos.

  2. Anti-gravity Room

    3. A room far from reality, where inside, you don't know what is right and what is wrong. The dimensions of the room create a new perception for the mind, defying the laws of physics and gravity. It's fun to capture souvenir photos in various positions, where your mind makes inconceivable inferences that can only be understood through experience, leaving you amazed.

  3. Head on the Platter

    4. Can you imagine cutting off your head and placing it on a platter?

    That's what happens with this trick, where your head appears alone on the platter while the rest of your body remains inside the box beneath the platter. It would be wonderful to capture a picture in this position.

  4. Beuchet Chair Illusion

    5. It's illogical for a person's size to change based on their location, but that's exactly what the Beuchet Chair does. The dimensions of the room deceive your mind, making it enjoyable to see a person sitting on this chair appear smaller. It represents a new experience in understanding perception, proportions, and dimensions.

pearl of Aswan

Description

Nile cruises are popular in Egypt, as Egyptians use small boats to transport their goods along the banks of the river. There are many types of Nile cruises from simple trips to luxury trips on steam ships or floating hotels, and there are more than 230 cruise ships on the Nile River.

One of the most important ways of Nile tourism in Egypt is the Nile Cruise between Luxor and Aswan.

Nile Cruises Floating on the Nile River is the perfect way to enjoy a sightseeing trip to Luxor and Aswan in southern Egypt, including sightseeing and impressive ruins along the way between the two cities.

The most important activities that you can do on the Luxor and Aswan Nile Cruise excursions

  • You can enjoy watching the landscapes on both sides of the Nile during your stay on the Nile Cruise Luxor and Aswan, where there are hills, mountains and green spaces.
  • In addition to enjoying the waters of the Nile, one of the longest and most beautiful rivers in the world.
  • On the banks of the Nile on the road from Luxor to Aswan, there are some famous attractions such as Botanical Island, Philae Garden, Abu Simbel Temple and others. You can enjoy visiting these places during your stay on the Nile Cruise.
  • Enjoy visiting the colorful Nubian houses and learn about the distinctive culture of the indigenous people of Aswan and Luxor, which is characterized by its delightful colors and simplicity of furniture.

  • Sonesta Nile Goddess Cruise

Egyptian Coast

Description

The North Coast is considered the favorite summer destination for Egyptians as well as foreigners, due to a number of reasons, the most important of which is the good weather there throughout the year, in addition to the presence of a large number of distinctive tourist resorts, it enjoys clear turquoise waters, soft white sand and many tourist projects that attract many customers.

Places of entertainment in the North Coast

Zahran City Park

Zahran City Park is one of the distinctive coastal outings. Being one of the famous commercial complexes in the region, it also includes many services, such as: the commercial market and a children's play area, as well as Zahran outdoor cinema, the latest films are also provided to be shown in the open night cinema for 50 pounds per person, and the children’s area includes some recreational games and green spaces that are surrounded by a number of restaurants and cafeterias.

Water sports cities in the North Coast

No one can imagine visiting the North Coast without experiencing water and beach sports and activities, perhaps the most fun and exciting of them is riding jet skis, banana boats and the vale board, and their best experiences are in Porto Marina.

Marseille Aquapark

The Jewel of the Red Sea

Description

The city of Sharm el-Sheikh is said to be the pearl of the Gulf, due to its bright waters with coral reefs, its mild atmosphere, and its sand dunes that sparkle in the desert. It is an integrated city where everyone who seeks recreation in the lush nature finds his demand, as well as those who search for shopping and a life full of recreational activities will inevitably find his demand. And the city of Sharm El-Sheikh contributes to raising the level of economy and tourism in Egypt, as it attracts thousands of tourists from all over the world, and it has obtained high positions in diving under the depths of the Red Sea, in addition to amazing safari trips.

The best entertainment places in Sharm El Sheikh

1. Aqua Blue and Water Park

One of the most beautiful recreational tourism destinations in Sharm El-Sheikh, as it is an ideal destination to take the family and children on an unforgettable leisure trip. the most famous of which are the black hole and kamikaze, in addition to nine swimming pools, including those suitable for children under 12 years old.

2. Dolphin and crocodile show

It is one of the strangest and most unique activities that you will enjoy during your tourism trip in Sharm El Sheikh. As your group trip organized by the hotel begins with three friendly and cute dolphins where you can swim, play, and take funny pictures with them. Followed by a second trip to see the giant Thai crocodile shows, shows playing with fire and live snakes, with the possibility of taking the strangest pictures of you while you are having fun with these frightening creatures in an unprecedented friendliness. And after you're done, you can take a tour of a museum dedicated to stuffed animals..

3. Alf Leila Wa Leila

One of the best places in Sharm El-Sheikh and the most attractive to tourists from all over the world; Alf Leila Wa Leila is closed entertainment complexe in the Umm Sid plateau area, which enjoys a special charm among its visitors because of the various services and activities it provides that satisfy all tastes, from cinema, sound and light shows to Egyptian folk dances, horse dances, and musical and artistic evenings. In addition to all of the above, Alf Leila Wa Leila provides you with a great opportunity to shop for all the wonderful gifts and souvenirs you need for your family, as well as for yourself, to keep that unforgettable trip immortal in your mind.

4. Soho Square

It is one of the most attractive places in Sharm El-Sheikh for visitors, and it includes these places.

5. Soho Square Shopping Mall

You can find souvenirs, international quality goods, the latest in clothing and accessories, as well as art galleries and home goods. This field is also located in the center of Savoy Resort, where White Knight Beach is located. You can enjoy and spend fun on this beach, practice squash, bowling, tennis, and then shop.

6. Khan El Khalili Bazaar

Khan El Khalili Bazaar in Soho is a miniature version of Khan El Khalili in Cairo; Both of them include a group of bazaars and shops for oriental souvenirs and handicrafts, as well as a group of distinctive popular restaurants.

Hollywood Sharm El Sheikh

One of the most areas in Sharm El-Sheikh that provides an atmosphere of joy, fun, and a bustling nightlife, and is perfectly suitable for taking the most beautiful photographs, accompanied by dancing fountains to the sounds of music and colorful lighting units. The place also includes an open theater that presents a singing or musical performance every night, in addition to a large number of high-end restaurants and cafes that provide their services to visitors. This is in addition to shops to buy everything you want, as Hollywood Sharm El Sheikh is a full-service place that provides you with entertainment and shopping at the same time.

Ghibli car racing

It is one of the best places of tourism in Sharm for lovers of the atmosphere of adventure, excitement and suspense in a place that is the only and largest of its kind in Sinai to practice car racing while providing all means of safety and comfort, including special uniforms and the latest racing cars.

Glass boat ride

One of the most wonderful activities that you can do during the tourism trip in Sharm El Sheikh is through a submarine that penetrates the borders of the clear sea of Sharm El Sheikh to see the most amazing sea creatures and colorful coral reefs below you through the glass bottom of the submarine.

the stable

One of the best places in Sharm El Sheikh, specifically in Naama Bay, which offers a new idea for entertainment and fun by enjoying the experience of horseback riding on the seashore. The stable is one of the tourist places in Sharm El-Sheikh that attracts the attention and visits of a large number of tourists coming to the city.

Ras Mohammed Reserve

One of the most famous landmarks of Sharm El-Sheikh and the most attractive to tourist delegations thanks to its diversity that appears in its topography and its unique marine creatures that spread over an area of 480 km,at the meeting point of the Gulfs of Aqaba and Suez, between the snow-white deserts and clear blue seas, it represents one of the best diving spots in the world as a whole. In addition to being an archaeological site, it also includes a group of sunken ship treasures. And through organized group snorkeling and diving tours in several major beaches in the Ras Mohammed Reserve, you can enjoy watching about 1,000 species of attractive colored fish, about 150 species of crustaceans and sea turtles, and hundreds of coral barriers with rock formations and picturesque color gradations, the most famous of which is the Shark Observatory, south of the reserve, with wonderful views of the sea.

Colored Canyon

One of the most beautiful tourist spots in Sharm El-Sheikh, for diving enthusiasts and enjoying lively and wonderful scenes of the unique marine life in the beautiful Sinai city. In the Colored Valley, you can see wonderful gradations of colorful rocky coral reefs. Do not miss visiting the wonderful valley with your own cameras for underwater photography, or renting one of them to enjoy the most beautiful moments and memorial photos for an unforgettable trip.

Sharm El Sheikh Safari

Safari is one of the best tourism activities in Sharm El Sheikh that you can do during your trip there. Sharm el-Sheikh group safari trips are organized through an agreement with one of the city's tourism companies, which will provide you with a bus to take you to and from your place of residence on a 3- to 4-hour trip between the beauty and tranquility of the desert. Where you can choose between several alternatives or do all of them if you like, accompanied by tour guides, between roaming in rented four-wheel drive cars or bikes, or walking between the valleys of the highlands. In addition to spending breaks in a group of Bedouin tents to enjoy an unparalleled traditional evening in everything from eating barbecue and traditional Bedouin tea by candlelight, to a variety of entertainment shows, including the famous folk Bedouin dances.
